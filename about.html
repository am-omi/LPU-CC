About LPU CampusCare

LPU CampusCare is a student-driven platform dedicated to creating a safe, efficient, and well-maintained campus environment at Lovely Professional University. Our mission is to empower students by providing them with a simple and accessible way to report issues around the campus, enabling prompt resolutions and fostering a collaborative community.

By using LPU CampusCare, students can report a range of campus issues, from broken infrastructure and cleanliness concerns to safety hazards and more. Our goal is to ensure a better campus experience for everyone by promoting proactive reporting and facilitating effective communication between students and campus management.

Our Mission

At LPU CampusCare, we believe in building a responsive and collaborative campus. Our platform is designed to:

  • Enhance campus safety and infrastructure through timely issue reporting.
  • Promote student engagement in campus maintenance and sustainability.
  • Facilitate communication between students and campus authorities for effective solutions.

Why Choose LPU CampusCare?

User-Friendly Reporting

Our platform offers a simple and intuitive interface, making it easy for students to report issues with just a few clicks.

Real-Time Updates

Students receive real-time updates on the status of their reported issues, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Recognition & Rewards

Active contributors are acknowledged through a monthly leaderboard, where students can earn points and virtual badges.
